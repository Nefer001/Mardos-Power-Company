What We Do

Comprehensive Power Services

🔄
Generator Synchronize System

Advanced paralleling and synchronization of multiple generator sets for seamless load sharing, redundancy, and maximum power reliability. We design, install, and commission synchronization panels for industrial and commercial clients.

01
🖥️
Software Diagnosis

Cutting-edge electronic diagnostics using OEM software for CAT, Perkins, Cummins, John Deere, and more. We read fault codes, calibrate sensors, update firmware, and deliver precise data-driven maintenance insights that prevent costly breakdowns.

02
⚙️
Generator Installation

Professional installation of generator sets of all sizes — from small residential standby units to large industrial power systems. We handle site assessment, civil works coordination, load calculations, and full commissioning.

03
🔧
Repair & Maintenance

Scheduled preventive maintenance and emergency repair services for all generator brands and models. Our mobile teams respond rapidly to minimise downtime and restore your power supply with lasting fixes.

04
🔁
Motor & Generator Rewinding

Expert rewinding of alternators and electric motors using quality copper wire and precision winding techniques. We restore original performance specifications, extending the service life of your equipment.

05
🔌
Electrical Works & More

Power factor correction, automatic changeover switch supply and installation, control panel design, and general electrical works. More services are continuously being added — contact us to discuss your specific needs.
